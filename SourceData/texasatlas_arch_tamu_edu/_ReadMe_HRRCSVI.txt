How to obtain the HRRC SVI:

1. Go to the Open Data Portal: https://texas-planning-atlas-tamu.hub.arcgis.com/
2. Under Download Data click on Demography and Social Vulnerability:
 https://texas-planning-atlas-tamu.hub.arcgis.com/search?tags=svi%2Cdemography
3. Under datasets click on Social Vulnerability Indicators_WFL1
4. Under Layers Select: SVI Tract 2020 and SVI Block Group 2020
5. For each layer click Download as .csv file

References:
HRRC (2025) Social Vulnerability Indicators WFL1: SVI Block Group 2020 and Tract 2020, Texas Planning Atlas. Retrieved from https://texas-planning-atlas-tamu.hub.arcgis.com/

Van Zandt S, Peacock WG, Henry DW, Grover H, Highfield WE, Brody SD (2012) Mapping social vulnerability to enhance housing and neighborhood resilience. Housing Policy Debate, 22(1): 29–55. https://doi.org/10.1080/10511482.2011.624528
